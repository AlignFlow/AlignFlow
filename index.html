Method Overview

Optional: add a pipeline figure inside this card.

Overview of the AlignFlow architecture. The observation is first encoded to parameterize an observation-conditioned Gaussian mixture noise model, which captures both discrete action modes and within-mode stochasticity.

During training, noise is sampled from all mixture components, and actions are generated via mode-conditioned flow matching, enabling the policy to learn diverse yet structured action distributions.

At inference time, a single latent mode is selected conditioned on the observation, and the corresponding flow generates a trajectory-consistent action, ensuring coherent decision making.
