Method Overview

Overview of the AlignFlow architecture. The observation is encoded to parameterize an observation-conditioned Gaussian mixture noise model, capturing discrete action modes and within-mode stochasticity. During training, noise is sampled from all mixture components and actions are generated via mode-conditioned flow matching. At inference, a single latent mode is selected conditioned on the observation, and the corresponding flow generates a trajectory-consistent action.
